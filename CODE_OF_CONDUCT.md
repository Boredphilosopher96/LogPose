# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

LogPose is committed to a welcoming, professional, and respectful community for contributors, maintainers, and users.

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

- Demonstrating empathy and patience
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback
- Respecting different viewpoints and experiences
- Prioritizing collaboration and accountability

Examples of unacceptable behavior include harassment, discrimination, intimidation, trolling, and personal attacks.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and may take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Reporting

Report incidents to `security@logpose.dev`.
